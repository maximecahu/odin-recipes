Smoked Beef

Decilicious

This recipe is the perfect one to prepapre in no time a delicious smoked beef meal that your family will enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Spices
  • Soy sauce

Steps

  1. Wash the beef using water and dry it with some paper towel.
  2. Add your salt and pepper.
  3. Add your spices and massage your beef to impregnate your spices into the beef.
  4. Pour a little bit of olive oil and soy sauce on your beef.
  5. Cook your beef in the oven at 220Â°C for 15 minutes.
  6. Serve!
