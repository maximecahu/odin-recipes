Smoked Chicken

Decilicious

This recipe is the perfect one to prepapre in no time a delicious smoked salmon meal that your family will enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Spices
  • Soy sauce

Steps

  1. Wash the chicken using water and dry it with some paper towel.
  2. Add your salt and pepper.
  3. Add your spices and massage your salmon to impregnate your spices into the chicken.
  4. Pour a little bit of olive oil and soy sauce on your chicken.
  5. Cook your chicken in the oven at 220Â°C for 15 minutes.
  6. Serve!
